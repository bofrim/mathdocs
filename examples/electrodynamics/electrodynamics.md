# Electrodynamics as a U(1) Gauge Theory

The gauge potential is a one-form. Its curvature is the electromagnetic field
strength.

$$
F = dA
$$

$$
F_{\mu\nu} = \partial_\mu A_{\nu} - \partial_\nu A_{\mu}
$$

## Gauge transformations

$$
A' = A + d\alpha
$$

$$
\psi' = e^{ie\alpha}\psi
$$

## Maxwell equations

$$
dF = 0
$$

$$
d\star F = \star J
$$
